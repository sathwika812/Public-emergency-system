Our Key Features

Help(Police)

When people gives a hand sign indicating "Help," the CCTV camera detects the gesture and automatically sends a signal, along with her GPS location, to the nearest police station. This rapid-response system ensures that help can reach her promptly, addressing the immediate need for assistance in potentially dangerous or emergency situations.

Help(Ambulance)

When an "Emergency" hand signal is detected, the CCTV camera recognizes the gesture and sends an alert with the GPS location to the nearest hospital. An ambulance is then dispatched to the location, ensuring rapid medical assistance in urgent situations
